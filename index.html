Why did you create this website?

I'm very picky when it comes to movies and tv shows. After hours, days, and even weeks of looking at movie and tv show trailers, I got bored of seeing the typical plot, which I know will lead to the typical ending. Most of these tv shows and movies are science fiction, but there are a few that aren't. These tv shows and movies are one of the best I've watched, and wanted others to know about them especially since since many of them are underrated. Although I'm one to avoid tv shows that have been cancelled, I did include some tv shows that have been cancelled. I included these tv shows because they are worth watching, and they left me wondering of what could've happened. I like movies that do more than just make me feel sad, happy, or scared, I like movies that make me think, make me wonder, and make me question.

If you've watched a movie or a tv show with a similar concept to any of these, comment them below.
